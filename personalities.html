Birsa Munda (1875–1900)

Freedom fighter and tribal leader who led the Munda Rebellion (Ulgulan) against British rule. Honored as “Dharti Abba”.

Jaipal Singh Munda (1903–1970)

Hockey captain of the 1928 Olympics gold-winning Indian team. Advocate for tribal rights and Jharkhand statehood.

Shibu Soren

Founder of Jharkhand Mukti Morcha, three-time Chief Minister, and key leader for Jharkhand's creation in 2000.

Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Legendary cricket captain from Ranchi. Led India to T20 2007, ICC 2011 World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy victories.

Deepika Kumari

World No.1 archer from Ranchi, representing India in multiple Olympics and inspiring young athletes globally.

Priyanka Chopra Jonas

Internationally recognized actress, singer, and humanitarian. Miss World 2000 and active in social causes through UNICEF.

Other Notable Personalities

  • Kariya Munda – Senior BJP leader and former Deputy Speaker of Lok Sabha.
  • Meira Kumar – Politician and diplomat; family roots in Jharkhand.
  • Ranchi Rays Players – Hockey players representing India internationally.
  • Artists & Folk Performers – Preserving tribal traditions in music, dance, and painting.
